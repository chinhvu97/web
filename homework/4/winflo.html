About Nike Winflo 8:

SECURE SUPPORT FOR FAST, EVERYDAY TRAINING.

When running is an everyday habit, you need support to match your speed. The Nike Winflo 8 brings back the cushioning you love and pairs it with details like a translucent upper and Flywire technology for a snug, stability fit for long runs.

Lightweight, Breathable Support

The upper uses translucent mesh and perforations at the forefoot for breathable comfort. The Flywire cables work with the laces to provide more support as you tighten them.

Cushioning For Your Miles

Zoom Air units in the forefoot and heel put cushioning where you need it. Cushlon foam makes it soft and responsive and puts more springiness in your step.
